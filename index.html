Musical Roots is a global musical collaboration for all people, novice to expert. Have you ever wanted to learn to play music? Are you a musical hobbyist? Are you a weekend warrior, a gig here and there every now and then? Semi-professional musician? Music teacher? Pro? Join Musical Roots to connect and improve your skills while helping a fellow musicians improve their skills. Join online jam sessions. View live concerts and much more!

Musical Roots will provide a specialized platform for musicians to connect, communicate, share music, collaborate on projects, mentor others, jam in practice rooms, rehearse in multi-track studios, audition for jobs and schools, perform online concerts, create portfolios, launch projects, create crowdfunders and more. Use Musical Roots to find and access the best music instrution on the web, the best blogs, forums and chats. Everything music is here!

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I get involved? It is simple just sign up and explore what we have to offer.
How much does it cost? Musical Roots is absolutely free for most users. There will be some premium services that are available to paid subscribers.
How can you offer such an excellent platform for free? We will have a minimal amount of paid advertising on select pages. We appreciate your support of our advertisers. The crowdfunding service will also generate income. We will receive 10% of the crowdfund proceeds.
Will there be certificates for completion of group instruction classes? No, we expect that the group classes are ongoing. There is no end to the class. As needed, we will start new cohorts at the beginning level. The idea is for you to get in where you fit in and learn what you want to know. You are free to move around to meet your specific needs, to meet new people and to contribute your talents.
Is this environment safe for children? Yes. When you sign up you will create a user name. Your personal information is private. In addition we have a jr. division for the children. When they login, they will be logging into their platform.
